Research Interests

Machine Learning in Bioinformatics

I am passionate about leveraging machine learning, particularly deep learning, to address complex problems in bioinformatics and computational biology. My focus lies in developing algorithms to analyze and interpret biological data with high precision, contributing to advancements in genomics and personalized medicine.

DNA Classification & Genomic Analysis

The classification and analysis of DNA sequences are central to my research. I aim to design innovative machine learning models that can decode patterns in genomic data, enhancing our understanding of genetic variations and their implications for health and disease.

PLM and AlphaFold

PLM (Protein Language Models) and AlphaFold represent cutting-edge advancements in protein structure prediction. PLM utilizes deep learning techniques to understand protein sequences and their functions, while AlphaFold leverages neural networks to predict protein folding with remarkable accuracy. My interest lies in exploring how these technologies can be integrated into bioinformatics workflows to enhance our understanding of protein interactions and their roles in biological processes.

Integration of AI and CRISPR Technologies

Combining AI with CRISPR gene-editing technology is accelerating advancements in medicine and agriculture. AI aids in identifying optimal gene-editing targets, improving precision, and reducing off-target effects. This synergy enhances the development of therapies for genetic diseases and the creation of resilient, high-yield crops.

High-Dimensional Data Analysis

Advancements in spatial omics and single-cell analysis are revolutionizing computational biology. By applying deep learning to these high-resolution datasets, I aim to uncover patterns in cellular processes and disease mechanisms.

Large Language Models in Genomics

Exploring the application of LLMs in genomic data processing fascinates me. These models offer potential in areas like DNA sequence annotation, functional prediction, and uncovering latent patterns in large-scale biological datasets.
